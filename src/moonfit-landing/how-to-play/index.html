How to start training with MoonFit

1
Connect Wallet

Connect to SubWallet with three easy steps.
If you haven't got one, install now.

2
Choose Your MoonBeast

Select between two types of MoonBeast: Beast and Beauty, either by their appearance or by their statistics. Each MoonBeast is unique with different strengths and weaknesses.

Level

High level means more stat points for each attribute

Social

Increase possibility when breeding

Luck

Affect how often you’ll get MoonBox during runs

Health

Less health = Less earning effectiveness

Endurance

Affect MoonBeast’s health after each training session

Speed

Better speed value = more tokens earned

3
Start Running

To start running, select your Beast, click Run > and start running. Make sure you running status is Ready so you can earn reward.

4
Earn Coin & Share

Once you finished your run, track your stats, see how you perform, and how much tokens you’ve earned. Share it with your friends. The more the merrier!

5
Run more,
Grow your clan

Keep running every day. You form a great habit and get more rewards as you run more. We believe it’s the long run that counts!

Use Earned tokens to upgrade, trade, hodl, sell or buy real fitness merchandise on MoonShop.
